About Our Wine

Winemaker – Julien Fayard

With more than two decades of winemaking expertise, Julien Fayard is one of California’s most respected winemakers producing American wine with French finesse. He believes in producing wines with an identity, exploiting what makes each one unique. Julien’s wines are a pure expression of their terroir, highlighting the specificities and characteristics of the fruit, vineyard management, soil and climate. He has a track record of building brands both in terms of quality and market presence. Originally from France, Julien began his career as a winemaker producing quality roséat his family’s winery in Provence. In Bordeaux he worked at Lafite Rothschild and Smith Haut Laffite before relocating to Napa Valley. In Napa, he was Philippe Melka’s director of winemaking for a few years before launching his own projects. Julien earned his Master in Agri-Business from ESA France and his Master in Winemaking from Toulouse France. In 2011, he received his MBA from UC Davis.

About Our Vineyard:

  • Located on the northwest facing hillside in the Stag’s Leap District
  • The vines were planted in 2018 after a devastating fire in 2017 wiped out the entire vineyard.
  • 100% cabernet sauvignon planted on just under 2 acres
